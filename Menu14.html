Pork Pomegranate

This recipe is Frence pork pomegranate.
Contains:pork, pomegtanate, cucumber, onion, parsley.




Ingredients in your box

*4 slices bread
*2 tsps butter
*1 thsp oil
*1/2 tsp cumin
*1 tsp garlic
*1 pinch turmeric
*salt
*2 green chilies

Ingredients your will need

*1 cup mixed veggies heaped (carrot, onion,corn)
*1 tomato
*1/2 to 3/4 tsp pav bhaji masala or any spice powder
*2 tbsp coriander leaves



Step-by-step Instructions

*Add oil to the pan, add cumin and allow to splutter. Saute garlic until a nice aroma comes.

*Add the veggies and to saute on a medium to high flame until hakf done or soft cooked to suit your tast.

*Next add masala powder and coriander leaves.

*Smear butter over the bread slices and toast them util golden on the outer slide and until crisp on the inner side. Transfer the prepared veggies. If desired add cheese at this step.
